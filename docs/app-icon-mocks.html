Snapforge — App icon concepts

20 directions for the macOS app icon. Each card shows the 128px hero icon plus 48px and 24px down-sampled previews so legibility at small sizes can be judged. Save this page to PDF (⌘P → Save as PDF) for offline review.

01 / aperture-classic
Aperture, classic
Six-blade iris with a red pupil. Reads as "capture" at every size; pupil doubles as the recording indicator.
02 / brackets-slash
Snap brackets
The current logo language refined: corner brackets + diagonal slash, with a record dot tucked into the top-right corner as a notification-style accent.
03 / shutter-button
Shutter button
Camera-app DNA: a tappable shutter button on a blood-red gradient. Strongest "press to capture" affordance.
04 / window-rec
Window with REC dot
macOS window chrome with traffic lights + a centered red record dot. Immediately reads as "screen capture" without needing a label.
05 / crosshair
Crosshair frame
Region-select metaphor: a frame with a dashed crosshair and a center dot. Strong "snipping tool" association.
06 / bolt
Snapforge bolt
Lightning-bolt mark on a sunset gradient. Reads as "fast capture / forge". Distinctive at any size.
07 / cassette
Cassette tape
Skeuomorphic cassette with red-pupiled reels. Plays into the "recording" theme; nostalgic without being kitschy.
08 / vinyl
Vinyl record
Black vinyl with a red label. The single black-on-black icon feels premium; the red center is the only chromatic anchor.
09 / polaroid
Polaroid
A tilted Polaroid with a red dot framed in its window. Warm, inviting, but the white background may clash with macOS Sonoma+ uniformity.
10 / filmstrip
Film strip
Vertical sprockets framing a single dark frame with a red dot. Reads as "movie / capture" cleanly even when shrunk.
11 / monogram-s
"S" monogram
A stylized white S with a red diagonal cut, evoking "snapforge" through letterform alone. Most word-mark adjacent.
12 / magnify
Magnifier with REC lens
Search/inspect metaphor; the red dot in the lens repurposes "look" as "record". Strong utility-app vibe.
13 / eye
Eye + red pupil
"You're being watched" iconography — fits a recording app explicitly. Slightly ominous; great if positioning is privacy-aware.
14 / forge
Forge anvil
Plays the "forge" half of the brand: an anvil with red sparks. Distinct from every other screenshot tool's iconography.
15 / scissors
Scissors / snip
Direct snip/clip metaphor. Universally legible, works as a small icon, but less unique than other directions.
16 / on-air
"ON AIR" sign
Broadcast aesthetic — quotes the studio sign. Clear at large sizes; the text collapses below ~32px so a 16/24px variant must replace text with a dot.
17 / stack
Stacked frames
A cascade of three captures with the active one (front, red dot) in focus. Speaks to the history/library feature too.
18 / pulse
Recording pulse
A static rendition of the menubar pulse: concentric red rings around a solid dot. Pure, abstract, minimal.
19 / camera-pixel
Pixel camera
Old-school camera body with a center lens. Small red rec-light pixel in the corner ties it to the menubar pill.
20 / quoted-dot
Quoted region
All four corner brackets enclosing a center red dot. Minimalist, mono palette, friendliest in light Sonoma+ Dock; reads instantly as "select region".
